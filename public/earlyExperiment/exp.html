This is the earliest experiment performed during the project, and is described in Section 4.1. of the writeup. We took the design of 5 existing online exhibitions, and replaced their exhibited objects with a subset of our own objects, in order to gain a better understanding of how different types of design work with our content. As a general rule, the 5 presented exhibitions are interactive when it comes to controls related to the objects (e.g. navigation between objects, zooming where it exists), but the links to other parts of the original websites have been inactivated, as we were only interested in the pages displaying the artworks.
