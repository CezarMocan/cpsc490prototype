This elegant figure wears a long cloak wrapped around his body, its sharp, diagonal creases emphasizing the linear strength of the composition. His helmet is of a type known as “Corinthian,” which is typical of the Archaic and Classical eras of ancient Greece, but the transverse orientation of its crest is striking and highly unusual.

Period
